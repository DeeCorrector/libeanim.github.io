Live Sudoku Solver

Description

The live sudoku solver uses computer vision technology and machine learning to detect a sudoku grid and project the solution to the live camera images.

First opencv is used to identify the outer bounds of the sudoku grid. After that the detected box is cut out and transformed to a 300x300 pixel square image. In the next step the small boxes containing the numbers are cut out and analysed by a previously trained support vector machine to recognize the digits. Now a simple sudoku solving algorithm calculates the solution of the sudoku and the missing numbers are projected into the image considering orientation and distortion of the original grid.
